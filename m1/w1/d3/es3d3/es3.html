Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizes topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!


Featured in:
Fresh Cranberry Recipes That GoBeyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (250g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 2 medium yellow onions. thinly sice
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrave rind off with a paring knife if desired, thought it's not neccessary
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
